Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken

    • Season the chicken with thyme, curry, garlic, ginger, salt, and seasoning cube.
    • Boil with a bit of water for 15–20 minutes (until tender).
    • Optionally, fry or grill the chicken after boiling.
    • Save the chicken stock — you’ll need it for the rice!

  2. Cook the Jollof Rice

    • In another pot, heat the vegetable oil.
    • Add sliced onion, stir until golden.
    • Add tomato paste, fry for 3 mins.
    • Add your blended pepper mix, fry for 10 mins.
    • Add thyme, curry, seasoning cubes, and bay leaf (optional).
    • Pour in the chicken stock (and water if needed).
    • Add washed rice, mix well.
    • Cover with foil or parchment paper and a lid to trap the steam.
    • Cook on low heat for 30–40 minutes, stir halfway to prevent burning.

  3. Fry Plantains

    • Peel and slice the ripe plantains diagonally.
    • Sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
    • Heat oil in a pan and fry until golden brown on both sides.
    • Drain on paper towels.

  4. Prepare the Jollof Base

    • Blend the tomatoes, red bell pepper, scotch bonnet, and 1 onion until smooth.
    • Pour into a pot and cook uncovered until the mixture reduces and loses water (about 10–15 mins).
